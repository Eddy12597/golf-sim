Simulator
In this simulation, there will be 18 holes (par=4) for Trump and Obama to play. Trump plays riskier: he prioritizes long shots (attempting to clear the lake) over short shots (landing before lake). Obama plays safer: if he is tied or worse, he chooses long shots; if he is leading, he chooses short shots.
